# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designers

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers shape the user experience and visual design of products. They conduct user research, create prototypes, and validate design solutions to ensure usability, accessibility, and alignment with user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design mockups
- Define user flows and interaction patterns
- Validate design solutions with users and stakeholders
- Collaborate with developers on implementation and design system consistency
- Document design decisions and patterns for team reference
- Ensure accessibility standards are met (WCAG compliance)

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support burden through great design
- Align design decisions with product strategy and user needs

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with product and engineering teams
- Usability testing findings and recommendations
- Design system documentation and component specs
- Collaboration in sprint planning for design-related tasks

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Aligns design direction with product goals; validates solutions through user feedback
- **Developers**: Partners on implementation, component handoff, and design consistency
- **Project Manager**: Coordinates design review gates and milestone dependencies

---

## Business Analysts

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business requirements and technical solutions. They elicit, document, and clarify stakeholder needs, then translate them into actionable requirements for the delivery team.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business and stakeholder requirements
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and needs assessments
- Define functional and non-functional requirements
- Create requirement traceability matrices and specifications
- Identify gaps between business goals and proposed solutions
- Support trade-off analysis and solution validation
- Document process improvements and lessons learned

### Goals
- Ensure solutions align with business objectives
- Reduce rework and scope creep through clear requirements
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction and project outcomes

### Typical Communication
- Requirement specification documents
- Stakeholder meeting facilitation and notes
- Trade-off analysis and decision documentation
- Collaboration with Product Managers and Project Managers

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborates on prioritization and feature validation
- **Project Manager**: Supports planning and requirement traceability
- **Developers**: Clarifies requirements and acceptance criteria during implementation
- **Stakeholders**: Acts as liaison to understand and communicate needs

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers own the infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and release automation. They enable reliable, repeatable deployments and provide observability and operational support.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage environments (dev, staging, production)
- Automate deployments and release processes
- Monitor system health, performance, and security
- Provide runbooks and incident response documentation
- Support infrastructure scaling and reliability
- Partner with developers on testing and deployment strategies

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, repeatable releases
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Provide visibility into system health and performance

### Typical Communication
- Release runbooks and deployment checklists
- Incident response coordination and post-mortems
- Infrastructure documentation and monitoring dashboards
- Collaboration with developers on CI/CD improvements

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Partners on pipeline optimization, testing infrastructure, and troubleshooting
- **Project Manager**: Coordinates release windows and deployment schedules
- **QA/Testing**: Supports test environment setup and automation
- **Security Lead**: Collaborates on security scanning and compliance controls in pipelines

---

## Customer Support

### Role Summary
Customer Support represents the voice of end-users. They provide feedback from customer interactions, surface recurring issues, and ensure the team is prepared to support customers at release.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and communicate customer feedback and issues
- Identify recurring problems and trends
- Participate in support readiness planning before releases
- Create support documentation (FAQs, troubleshooting guides)
- Collaborate on incident triage and resolution
- Advocate for customer needs in product and project decisions
- Train internal teams on product features and support workflows

### Goals
- Deliver excellent customer experience and satisfaction
- Reduce support burden through proactive issue resolution and clear documentation
- Improve product quality through early identification of issues

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and issue trends
- Support readiness checklists and documentation
- Release announcement coordination
- Customer escalation reporting and resolution tracking

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Provides customer feedback to inform prioritization
- **Developers**: Reports bugs, supports troubleshooting, and validates fixes
- **Project Manager**: Coordinates release readiness and support planning
- **QA/Testing**: Collaborates on issue reproduction and validation

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads define and enforce security requirements, conduct threat assessments, and ensure compliance across projects. They partner with teams to integrate security into every stage of delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Define security requirements and threat models for projects
- Conduct security design reviews and code reviews
- Manage security scanning tools and vulnerability assessment
- Create and maintain security incident response playbooks
- Support compliance and regulatory requirements
- Conduct security training and awareness
- Triage and prioritize security findings and fixes
- Partner with teams on secure coding practices

### Goals
- Reduce security vulnerabilities and compliance risk
- Enable teams to build secure systems by design
- Maintain customer trust and regulatory compliance

### Typical Communication
- Security requirement specifications and threat models
- Vulnerability reports and remediation plans
- Incident response coordination and post-mortems
- Security training and awareness documentation
- Compliance audit reports and remediation tracking

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Partners on secure coding practices and code review feedback
- **Project Manager**: Ensures security gates are included in project milestones
- **DevOps Engineer**: Collaborates on security scanning in CI/CD, infrastructure hardening, and incident response
- **Product Manager**: Advises on security trade-offs and risk acceptance decisions
- **QA/Testing**: Coordinates security testing and penetration testing coordination
